Working in research on a technical engineering position means working along teams of highly passionate researchers and defining the most appropriate solutions to achieve their objectives.

We were working in consolidating the european geolocation systems in the Southern Alps along with the galileo teams. As the originals stations were built independently each of them had a specific energy system ( often solar powered batteries ) and data transmission system ( everything from wifi to bluetooth and radioâ€¦ ).

Now, each of these systems had its own specifications and it was a burden for the teams to work in these conditions, the Alps being rough terrain, telecommunications are not efficient and when a remote antenna fails, it could mean working on rocky peaks that require an helicopter.

The project was to take advantage of the recent IoT protocols to build an array of antennas, with a single centralized main unit that would transmit data to the research centre and only small replicators along the antennas to extend the coverage. To implement this system i chose is based on zigbee network and raspberry pi controllers.

This idea would solve all maintenance problems and significantly reduce the cost of the installations.
