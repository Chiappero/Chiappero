Airbus Helicopters' IT department is large and has countless entry points. I was presented with a situation where each department were cascading to each of its product and service lines the tasks produce specific standardized KPIs. The resulting workload was estimated at one day of full-time work per employee per month, at an estimated cost of more than 1 million euros per year.

My first proposal was to upgrade the current tooling, consisting of excel and powerpoint, to fully integrate the data into a single SAP database. I have chosen to use Business Object as an analysis and Business Intelligence solution. The project aimed to consolidate all the different data sources and to automate the generic tasks related to KPI calculations. Its scope included human resources, finance, projects and execution. It took several deep dives into each department's processes to master them and automate them into a single solution.

The project was gradually deployed, starting with governance, which were the actors with the most needs, and ending with operations that were the most technical and deeply linked to other tools and contracts.

Dashboards no longer have any risk of human error, misinterpretation or misinformation, the workload has gone from astronomical to non-existent and the frequency of updates is now daily (whereas it was monthly). The management and the board of directors thus have at their disposal a piloting and decision support solution.
