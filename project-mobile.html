During my experience at Airbus, I was also very involved in the development of a non-profit Junior-Enterprise.

Junior Enterprises are a specific type of non-profit structures. Ils sont situés dans des établissements d'enseignement supérieur et visent à offrir des solutions professionnelles basées sur les compétences théoriques enseignées par l'école.

I was recruited as a chargé d'affaires and my first responsibilities were to be the focal point for client and to manage the team of developers working on their projects.

after a year and 4 successful client’s project I was elected head of Business department, my responsibilities were to manage all the activities from prospecting to customer retention. This scope include all commercial and project management activities such as contract negotiation, resource management, deliverable realization, team management.

When I started we were struggling in a dilemma situation : We were having difficulty positioning ourselves on offers as a result our teams faced exhaustion and demotivation from that. My first action was to rework our prospection model, to define specific buyer persona and to adapt our actions. I also strengthened our entire process to improve our conversion rate in the pre-sales phase. In the span of three month we achieved total revenues equal to that of the previous year.

I identified the key actors and analysed their stakes and needs. I then designed an architecture based on web and cloud technologies, angularJS and J2EE webservices . The idea was to have a single database synchronized with SAP PPM in order to manage evolutions, track changes, drive operations on budget. The technologies were chosen to allow for portability and maintainability.

The development took less than three month, with frequent exchange with stakeholders to ensure their implication and satisfaction. As of today the product as received several very positive feedback from both business and IM users, it saves several working days to the department and allow for a better relationship with businesses.

This example is one of the many projects I have carried out, I would be happy to go into more detail in an interview.
